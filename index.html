Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV)

By: Alessia Beck

HIV is a virus that attacks immune cells rapidly. It is spread through body fluids that go after the immune system attacking CD4 cells called T cells. This does not allow the human body to fight diseases and infections. HIV is an ongoing epidemic in the United States that can be transmitted sexually, perinatally and from blood transmission. On going research is constantly being done to find the permanent cure for this life threatnening disease.
